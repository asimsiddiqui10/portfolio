SportTriad

Founding AI Engineer.

SportTriad is an early-stage platform focused on the mental and emotional development of young athletes. It helps players, parents, and coaches build healthier habits around confidence, reflection, and communication - not just performance.

SportTriad is designed around a privacy-first knowledge graph that models relationships between athletes, reflections, habits, and contextual signals over time, rather than relying on raw conversational history. Sensitive data is stored as structured, permissioned nodes with explicit ownership and visibility rules across athlete, parent, and coach roles. The knowledge graph enables longitudinal insight and personalization while minimizing data exposure, and AI systems operate only on scoped, derived representations rather than direct access to raw user content. This architecture emphasizes auditability, least-privilege access, and long-term trust, which are critical when building systems for youth users.

marketise.ai

Frontend Engineer + AI

Marketise.ai is an AI-driven marketing platform that helps brands analyze, optimize, and create digital presence assets by leveraging machine learning and automated workflows. Built with scalable backend services and modular AI components, the system ingests brand profiles or URLs and produces structured insights about visual identity, messaging, and aesthetic cues that can inform design and content decisions. I also worked on architecture improvements to support maintainability and horizontal scalability as usage grows, with a strong focus on modular service design and observability in production.

Soccertact

Product design | UI/UX

I designed a scouting dashboard focused on helping users quickly understand player information without cognitive overload. The work was driven by user research and regular feedback from scouts to understand how they scan data, what they trust visually, and where friction exists in existing tools. The goal was to surface the right information at the right moment, reducing unnecessary complexity while keeping depth accessible when needed.

From a design perspective, I focused on clear information hierarchy, consistent spacing, and readable typography to support fast decision-making. Layouts and components were designed and iterated in Figma, with attention to alignment, color usage, and interaction states across different screen sizes. The final UI emphasizes clarity, consistency, and usability, ensuring the dashboard feels intuitive and reliable in real scouting workflows.
